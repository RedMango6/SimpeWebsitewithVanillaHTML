Short Summary

Hello, my name is Taha Iqbal. I was born on February 27 2003 in Toronto, Ontario. I am a Muslim and am from an Indian Heritage. My parents are from the Biharre province of India (a north-east province). I am currently a full time Computer Science (Co-op) student at Ryerson in my second year. I plan to graduate in 2025.

After being born in 2003, I was raised by my loving mother in our toronto apartment for two years. After this 2 years, we moved to Misissauga and my little brother was born. My little brother was name "Talha Iqbal". He has a similar name to me. While we lived in Mississauga, my mother eventually got preganant with her third son. The third son (my second little brother) was born in 2008. He was named Tahir. When I was in grade 4, my father moved the family from Mississauga to Oakville.

For middle school, I went to River Oaks Public School. For high school, I went to White Oaks Public School. I spent my last two years of high school suffering from the pandemic. :(
